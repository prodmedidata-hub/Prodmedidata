Turning healthcare supply data into smarter, faster, cost-saving decisions.

What We Do

Meditech is an AI-driven platform that streamlines the healthcare supply chain for hospitals, suppliers, and GPOs. By combining real-time data, a global product database, and predictive analytics, we help reduce costs, optimize inventory, and improve patient outcomes.

Core Offerings

For Providers

  • MT-Global Product Database – Perpetually updated catalog.
  • MT-Spend Analysis – AI-driven cost & supplier optimization.
  • MT-CQOR – Integrates cost, quality, outcomes, reimbursements.
  • MT-Procedure Card Management – OR supply optimization.
  • MT-Robotic Surgery Analysis – Tracks & optimizes robotics use.

For Suppliers & GPOs

  • MT-Kit Quote – Automates surgical kit creation & pricing.
  • MT-Contract Management – Digital supplier agreements.
  • MT-ORB – GPO-in-a-box for rapid setup.
